What makes us different

The difference is in the details.

Most padel retreats take anyone who books. The result? Mismatched levels, frustrated players, and a week that never quite finds its rhythm.

La Pausa works differently. Every guest applies. We review each application and assign players to the retreat and group that fits them — by level, by style, by what they're looking for. So when you arrive, everyone belongs there.

Your head coach

Héctor Perona.

71 titles. A World Padel Tour career. Brought to every session at La Pausa.

A former Top 40 World Padel Tour player and #1 ranked player in both Spain and Andalusia, Héctor has competed and won at every level — 2 World titles, 16 national championships, and over 50 regional titles across his career.

Beyond his playing record, he is one of Spain's most respected coaches, having trained Paquito Navarro — one of the greatest padel players of all time — alongside many other elite players. At La Pausa, he brings that same high-performance approach to players of all levels.
